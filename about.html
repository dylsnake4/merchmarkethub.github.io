About Us

Founded in the United States, Merch Market Hub LLC was built by operators who understand the logistics, velocity, and compliance needed to win on modern marketplaces. Our team brings years of experience in e-commerce, supply chain, and vendor management to every partnership.

We don’t just buy products — we build relationships. We prioritize vendors who align with our values: honesty, accountability, and mutual profitability.

Our operations are rooted in long-term thinking. We invest in technology, workflow automation, and process development to make sure our supplier relationships run smoothly from day one.

We have built trust by paying on time, ordering consistently, and working collaboratively with brands to protect and enhance their online presence. Whether it’s compliance with MAP pricing or rapid sell-through strategies, we operate as a strategic extension of your sales team.
