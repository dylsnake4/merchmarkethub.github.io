Our Process

We make working with us efficient and reliable. Here’s what you can expect:

  • 1. Supplier Approval: We’ll review your catalog, policies, and MAP enforcement before onboarding.
  • 2. Order Placement: We issue purchase orders based on your MOQ and availability.
  • 3. Fulfillment: We coordinate shipment to our fulfillment centers with full transparency.
  • 4. Payment: We pay on time — every time. We offer fast payment cycles and no surprises.
  • 5. Reorder Cycles: Our systems forecast demand and issue timely reorders based on your inventory and trends.

We believe that a well-structured process leads to less friction and higher ROI for everyone involved. That's why we provide onboarding documentation, automated PO generation, and detailed reorder reporting.

If you’re a distributor or brand interested in scalability and reliability, our process was built for you.
